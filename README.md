# Prayag Engineering - Industrial Portfolio

A professional, modern, and high-performance single-page website developed for **Prayag Engineering**. This platform showcases expertise in mechanical fabrication, heavy industrial erection, and specialized piping services.

---

## 🏭 About the Project
Prayag Engineering is a leader in industrial site services. This website was built to bridge the gap between heavy mechanical expertise and a modern digital presence. It focuses on clarity, safety standards, and project reliability.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Modern Industrial Aesthetic**: A premium dark theme featuring smooth gradients and curated typography that reflects engineering precision.
* **Dynamic UX**: Parallax scrolling effects and subtle micro-animations to enhance user engagement.
* **Service Catalog**: Comprehensive breakdown of core competencies:
    * Mechanical Fabrication & Erection
    * High-Pressure Piping (CS & SS)
    * Boiler, Turbine, & STG Services
    * Annual Maintenance & Shutdowns
* **Systematic Workflow**: A 4-stage visualization of the project lifecycle: **Assessment → Fabrication → Erection → Certification.**
* **Mobile-First Design**: Fully responsive layout optimized for all devices, from site tablets to desktop monitors.
* **Functional Contact Form**: Registration-free integration via **FormSubmit.co** for seamless quote requests.

## 🛠 Technologies Used

* **HTML5**: Semantic structuring for SEO and accessibility.
* **CSS3 (Vanilla)**: Custom styling, layouts, and industrial-grade animations without heavy libraries.
* **JavaScript**: Handles dynamic UI behaviors, scroll reveals, and form validation.

## 📊 Business Logic & Content

### Specialized Services
* **Mechanical Works**: Heavy structural fabrication and equipment alignment.
* **Piping Systems**: Steam and process piping including hydro testing.
* **Maintenance**: Preventive, breakdown, and emergency repair services for industrial plants.

### Industries Served
The project highlights Prayag's reach across:
* 🔥 Power Plants (Thermal & Biomass)
* ⛽ Oil & Gas / Refineries
* 🏗️ Steel & Cement Plants
* 🥛 FMCG & Food Processing

## ⚙️ Maintenance & Configuration
The website is designed for easy updates:
* **Centralized Details**: Easily modify contact info, GST numbers, and service descriptions from a central config.
* **Scalable Architecture**: Easily add new sections like "Vision & Mission" or "Project Gallery."
